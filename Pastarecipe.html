Pasta Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz. pasta (such as penne or fettuccine)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
Instructions:
  1. Cook the pasta according to package directions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the flour and whisk until smooth.
  3. Gradually add the milk, whisking constantly to prevent lumps from forming.
  4. Add the salt, black pepper, and garlic powder, and continue to whisk until the sauce thickens.
  5. Remove the saucepan from the heat and stir in the grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Pour the sauce over the cooked pasta and toss until well coated.
  7. Serve hot, garnished with additional grated Parmesan cheese and chopped parsley, if desired.
    8. Enjoy your delicious White Sauce Pasta!
